Something's fishy around here... Could it be!? We're announcing a new project!

## \[PS2\] Space Fishermen

![Space Fishermen screenshot](/images/blog/0060/01_fishermen.png)

Official U.S. Playstation Magazine subscribers of yesteryear, the time has finally come for the full English release of Space Fishermen! Featuring a soundtrack composed by the master, Soichi Terada (Ape Escape) this underground gem on the PS2 has all the wet fishing action your life has been missing. The Lords of the galaxseas have finally shown themselves and it's up to three brave fishermen to end their terrifying reign over the Space Fisherman's Association fishing spots! With over 120 space fish to catch, you'll be gone fishing for a while! Due for release...when it's done...

## \[PS2\] Fullmetal Alchemist 3: The Girl Who Succeeds God

![Fullmetal Alchemist 3: The Girl Who Succeeds God screenshot](/images/blog/0060/02_fma.png)

Of course, starting a new project doesn't mean we're neglecting our older ones. In fact, there's been major developments on FMA3, including the fact we've now been able to add subtitles to the cutscenes! We're currently in the middle of our final checklist, and hope to have the patch released in the not-so-distant-future!

## \[NDS\] Death Note: Successors to L

![Death Note: Successors to L screenshot](/images/blog/0060/03_deathnote.png)

Great progress has also been made on Successors to L, as all known bugs have now been fixed! The game is currently taking a bit of a backseat as we work to get the FMA3 patch released, but we're looking forward to getting back to it soon.
