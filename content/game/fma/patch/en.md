## Rom Patcher

Please select your dumped _Fullmetal Alchemist 3_ `.iso` ROM file, choose the options you'd like to use, then click "Patch ROM" to apply the patch.

This process may take a few minutes to complete.

### Compatibility Notes

---
icon: fa6-solid:triangle-exclamation

Due to the game's large file size, the online patcher will only run on Firefox. For other browsers, please refer to the manual patching method in the [setup guide](/fma/guide/ps2/patching-the-rom#patching-manually).
